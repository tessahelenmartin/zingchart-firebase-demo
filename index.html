The Surf Report

A ZingChart-Firebase Boilerplate Demo

The chart on the left displays current surf trends for Torrey Pines. The x and y axes correspond to time/date and wave height (ft) respectively. The direction of the arrows represents wave direction. The scale of the arrows relates to the period of the waves (s). On the right is a visualization of the selected wave height on a 6ft tall man with a 9ft surfboard next to him.
